Jordan A. Murillo

I just graduated from the International Master’s Program of Applied Economics and Social Development at National Chengchi University in Taipei, Taiwan. My research interests are in political economics and econometrics.

Originally from Belize, I have been living in Taiwan for more than 8 years now. In that time, along with my Master’s, I have attained a BSc in Management and Finance from National Tsing Hua University in Hsinchu, Taiwan, as well as professional proficiency in Mandarin Chinese (熟能生巧！).

During my studies, I interned for more than a year as a data analyst at the Taipei Financial Center Corp. (Taipei 101) in the marketing department for the mall, serving world-famous brands as clients.

I’m personally interested in reading, film, and fitness!
